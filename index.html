Birth

Kieran Ramirez was born on February 17th, 1988, in Alexandria, Virginia, to David Marcus Ramirez and Elena Ramirez. His father was a civil engineer and his mother was a college history professor.

Life

Kieran Ramirez had a difficult early life. His mother suffered from a chronic illness, and his father was always at work, requiring him to work extensive hours to provide for the family in Elena's absence. Consequently, Kieran spent most of his days in daycare, as his mother's bedridden condition rendered her unable to provide primary care. At the age of 7, his mother passed away from complications from cancer, so he was left with his father. His father, suffering from depression and struggling at work to keep a roof over their heads, placed significant pressure on him after his mother passed. This put some stress on Kieran.

Kieran wasn’t academically exceptional, but he always tried to meet his father’s expectations. He didn’t enjoy school and didn’t bother attending any extra activities. He did, however, develop a profound interest in military service and military history. His father noted that this interest in history would have made his mother, the professor, very proud.

Upon graduating high school in June of 2006, he enlisted in the US Army and promised his father he’d come back alive. This was, unfortunately, their final exchange. His father was killed in a workplace accident, falling over 100 feet after a piece of plywood scaffolding snapped beneath him. Kieran selected the 11X MOS, aspiring to join Special Operations. He excelled, graduating at the top of his class in Infantry School. He was subsequently assigned to Alpha Company, 1st Battalion, 75th Ranger Regiment, serving his full four year contract. He participated in seven operations on foreign soil, all of which were classified as successful.

The Incident

On his final deployment, Ramirez's team executed a raid on a compound outside Kandahar, suspected of housing a high-value insurgent leader. However, when they arrived, the compound was clear of insurgent forces. They found something else entirely: an Anomalous Entity manifesting as a coherent, mobile shadow. Standard firearms proved ineffective. The team deployed non-lethal deterrents, finding that a high-lumen flashbang caused the entity to dissipate.

Soon, Agents from BoPC responded after hijacking local radio communications. They detained the Ranger team and administered Class-A amnestics to all personnel involved. Kieran, however, displayed an anomalous resistance to the standard amnestic treatment for reasons that remain unknown. Given his unique resilience, combat expertise, and psychological profile, BoPC extended an alternative. He could undergo a significantly higher, experimental dose of amnestics, or he could join the Bureau. His service record would be altered to list him as "Killed in Action." Given he had nothing to lose and found interest in this secret organization, he accepted their offer and was inducted into combative work.

During his service in BoPC, Agent Ramirez helped in the containment of 14 Paranormal Based Anomalies across the United States. He was successful in 13, with the exception of one, as detailed in Medical Log BPC-ML-2023.05.19-19452.

Afterwards, he was transferred to a special unit designated SOG-3 “Hammerfall.” This is a Special Operations Group assigned to elite, heavy-duty operations for the Bureau, as well as excelling in the protection of assets. To date, Kieran still works in SOG-3, has gone on to complete two more operations under his belt, and is seen as the Bureau’s Ironfist.

MEDICAL LOG: BPC-ML-2023.05.19-19452

Attending Physician: Dr. Russell Vaz

Patient: Agent Kieran Ramirez

Summary

Patient suffered a run in with a paranormal anomaly known as “SPECTRE-17.” This occurred while Agent Ramirez was attempting to subdue and contain the anomaly. During the engagement, the entity threw a butcher knife at Kieran, severing his left arm clean off.

Details

Agent Ramirez passed out after losing too much blood and was rushed to a nearby medical facility, where they treated the trauma and stabilized his condition. After BoPC recovered Ramirez from the medical facility, he volunteered for Project: PROMETHEUS. Using technology reverse-engineered from Prometheus Labs, a prosthetic arm was surgically connected to Kieran. The prosthesis interfaces directly with his nervous system, granting him full somatic control.

The arm is extremely durable, being created from an anomalous material called “Veralux.” The augmentation also provides Agent Ramirez with extremely increased strength and accuracy. The prosthesis does not require an external source of energy.
